Search Quality Evaluation

Provide enhanced search results for users all over the world

Great search results and savvy matching algorithms rely on human feeback

Use case

Providing quality search results goes beyond basic keyword and results matching. High quality search results require understanding natural language queries, user intent, and the local market of the user. Without this understanding, users receive receive poor matches that don’t meet their needs.

Our approach

eDataFarm assists its clients in improving the search experience by providing enhanced machine learning training data that generates accurate search results. Whether it’s reviewing whole pages or specific queries, we can design a customized program to improve your overall search relevance.

 

Why eDataFarm?

Since most of our search experts in Sub-Saharan Africa (SSA) are outsourced locally, they’re familiar with search trends, popular and obscure topics, and the linguistic nuances of your target users. These specialists are experts at correctly identifying the user intent for search queries and the level of satisfaction a particular query result is likely to provide.
